# Separate Organizer Onboarding From Trip Creation

TripOS will treat Organizer onboarding and Trip creation as separate product flows. Organizer onboarding ends once a User creates an Organizer, becomes its first Owner, and sets enough Organizer Identity to enter the Operations Dashboard; creating the first Trip is a prominent next action from Organizer Home, not an onboarding gate.

This favors an Organizer-first SaaS structure over the faster activation path of forcing a first Trip during onboarding. The Operations Dashboard can exist for Organizers with zero Trips, while Trip creation remains an Owner-only workflow under the Trips area and public booking remains blocked by payment method readiness rather than by onboarding state.
